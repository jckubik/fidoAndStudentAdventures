About Fido&Student Adventures

Fido&Student Adventures was created as a way for students in the Blacksburg region to find more adventures to take with their best pals. It's our hope to share our love for dogs and foster a dog friendly community in Blacksburg.


Fido&Student Adventures was created by Jacob Kubik.
